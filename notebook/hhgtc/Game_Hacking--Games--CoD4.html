### Pitch and Yaw


From 0,0
the pitch range is 85 (straight down) to 275 (straight up)
and the yaw range is 0 to 360 (from 0 rotating right)

However, the pitch and yaw in CoD4 is cumalative. There's no max pitch and yaw.
Rotate to the right twice and you'll be at 720 degrees yaw.
