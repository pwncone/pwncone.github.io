# Not get banned


Random stuff I've seen people do to not get banned.
I have no idea if any of these work or not.

## Seed a random number


I've seen people seed and generate a random number at the start of their code.
This will change the hash of your code, but pattern/signature detection
should still realise this is the same code.

Seen in Gods-Eye Reborn (Modern Warfare Warzone cheat)
https://github.com/hailzfn/IGodsEye-Reborn/blob/d100a996be58cf7751a5fc6c74850d788ea52c43/Application.cpp#L16

srand((unsigned int)std::time(nullptr));


## Don't exceed the maximum pitch & yaw


In games like CSGO, if you're using an aimbot
and exceed that maximum pitch and yaw of your current player to aim at the target,
you'll get an instant ban.
