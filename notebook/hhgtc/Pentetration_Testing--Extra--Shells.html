## Tips


• ALWAYS TRY TO CONNECT BACK TO YOURSELF ON PORT 80 OR PORT 443 FIRST
◇ It will save you a ton of hassle if the target system is running behind a firewall and you're not able to get your shells back because you're trying them port 9001, WHICH IS BLOCKED YA NUMPTY.
◇ If this fails, PING YOURSELF to verify that you can access your attacking host
