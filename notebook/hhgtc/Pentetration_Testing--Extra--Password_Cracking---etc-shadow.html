# Password Cracking - /etc/shadow


There's many options to crack /etc/shadow passwords.

The format of the hashes in /etc/shadow is:
$ID$SALT$ENCRYPTEDPASS

$1 = md5
$5 = sha-256
$6 = sha-512

## Crack


### john


To crack /etc/shadow hashes with john, you need to combine the /etc/passwd and /etc/shadow files with unshadow:
unshadow passwd-file.txt shadow-file.txt
unshadow passwd-file.txt shadow-file.txt > unshadowed.txt
john --rules --wordlist=/usr/shre/wordlists/rockyou.txt unshadowed.txt
