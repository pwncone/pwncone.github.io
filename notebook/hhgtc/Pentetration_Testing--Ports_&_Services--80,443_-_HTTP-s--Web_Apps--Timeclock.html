# Timeclock


http://timeclock-software.net/
Timeclock is an employee time and attendance system that keeps track of when your employees sign in and out of work.

Examples
• oscp 10.11.1.252 - fw_dev

## Exploits


• https://www.exploit-db.com/exploits/39404 - Lots of SQL inject vulnerabilities
• https://cxsecurity.com/issue/WLB-2016010155 - Blind SQL injection
