# Linux - Commands - Job control



Ctrl-C - interrupt process
Ctrl-Z - suspend process

& - background a job
fg % - foreground a job

<command>& - run command in the background
jobs - view all backgrounded jobs
• + next to the job ID means that it is the most recent background job that started.
• - is the second most recent command.
fg %<job ID> - foreground a job
kill %<job ID> - kill a background job

Ctrl-Z > bg - suspend a job and send it to the background
